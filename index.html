The Procedure

A quiet, cinematic day. A lasting, natural result.

The ARTAS iXi performs the follicular harvest with a precision human hands cannot match. Dr. Khanna remains the artist, designing the hairline and placing every graft one at a time. The procedure typically takes 6 to 9 hours in a single session, and most patients typically return to work and activities within a week.

01
Design

Dr. Khanna maps a hairline specific to your face shape, hair direction, and facial maturity. A digital template is generated for the ARTAS robot.

~45 min
Private consult room
02
Robotic harvest

ARTAS selects the healthiest follicles at 44 microns and extracts them with circular punches so small they leave tiny dot scars, never a line.

3 to 5 hours
Local anesthesia
03
Hand placement

Dr. Khanna places each graft by hand, controlling angle, depth, and density so new hair grows in the same direction as the hair next to it.

2 to 4 hours
Hand work, by Dr. Khanna
04
Recovery

You typically rest for 2 days, wash carefully on day 3, and typically return to work and regular activities by day 5. Grafts secure in 10 to 14 days. Full density is visible at 12 months.

We back every graft. If any transplanted follicle does not grow, we replace it at no additional cost. This is rare with ARTAS, but we stand behind our work.

ARTAS robotic FUE is a well-established, minimally invasive procedure, but like any medical procedure it carries some risks. The most common side effects are temporary and mild: swelling, redness, scabbing, itching, and numbness in the donor or recipient area that typically resolves within a few weeks. Rare risks include infection, folliculitis, temporary shock loss of surrounding hair, uneven growth, or grafts that do not take. Dr. Khanna will review your full medical history and discuss every risk in detail during your private consultation before you decide to move forward.
